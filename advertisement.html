Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about advertising

How quickly can my ad go live?

Once payment is confirmed and ad materials are approved, your advertisement can go live within 24 hours.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, mobile money, and online payments. Payment details are provided after your ad request is approved.

Can I change my ad content during the campaign?

Yes! Premium and Enterprise packages allow up to 3 content changes during the campaign period.

Do you provide performance reports?

All packages include basic analytics. Premium and Enterprise packages include detailed performance reports and insights.

What ad formats do you support?

We support images (JPG, PNG), animated GIFs, and for Enterprise packages, we can accommodate video content as well.

Is there a discount for student businesses?

Yes! We offer a 20% discount for verified student-owned businesses. Contact us for more information.
